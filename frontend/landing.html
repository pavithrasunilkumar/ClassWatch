How it works

Up and running
in minutes

No hardware installation. No IT department. Just a laptop with a camera and a browser.

1
Point your camera
Plug in any USB or laptop camera facing the classroom. ClassWatch works with standard webcams — no special hardware required.
2
Start a session
Log in, click Start Session. The AI pipeline activates immediately — detecting students, tracking attention in real time on your GPU.
3
Watch the dashboard
See live attention percentages, get alerted when class focus drops, and watch the timeline update frame by frame.
4
Export your report
End the session to get a full PDF or CSV report — average attention, peak moments, distraction events, stability score.
Features

Everything a teacher needs.
Nothing they don't.

Real-Time Attention %
Live gauge showing exactly what fraction of the class is attentive right now. Updates every frame.

Get notified the moment class attention drops below your threshold — so you can re-engage before it's too late.

Privacy First

We never store
a single face.

ClassWatch is built from the ground up with student privacy as a hard constraint — not an afterthought.

Faces are blurred in the video feed — no identifiable footage ever leaves the camera.
Only aggregate attention data is stored — we log percentages, not individuals.
All processing is local — your camera feed never leaves your machine.
Head pose, not face recognition — we detect attention direction, not identity.
